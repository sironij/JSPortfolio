Handsup connects music lovers directly with their favourite artists, reimagining the concert experience.

Live concerts are unique moments that will remain forever, wouldn't be great if you can live the same experiance through your digital devices? HandsUp is the new app that will make it possible thanks to its features focused on sharing emotions live. To run this service is been created a mockup for a mobile and a smart tv app. The system stream the concert from artist but also the interaction between participants.
