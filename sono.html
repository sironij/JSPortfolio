TEAM: Arianna Agudio, Matteo Garagiola, Michele Pedrazzini, Tea Riva


sòno is an area of experimentation that allows you to perceive through sound one's own personal space and someone else's. A system tracks users' positions by creating a virtual environment where the sound is spatialized and increases or decreases as the distance between participants varies.

The sense of proxemic space qualifies us as human, but we often perceive it unconsciously. The sound translates it and allows the perception to be isolated.
In addition to the production of specific sounds that identify each user, the experience has been digitally translated on a website that adds in-depth information about concept. The tracks have been published on Spotify and a polystyrene sound box has been built to recall the motif of the visual.
