Liquida is a magazine related to the category of social publishing. The starting theme is that of discomfort, applied to the social theme of integration, especially in the Italian context.

The discomfort linked to cultural integration is presented in a transformative perspective as a catalog of stories of heterogeneous people living in the city. The subject of the magazine is therefore the city identity built by a “liquid society” in which everyone is free to be himself, without cultural differences or rather where the difference is the founding element of a dynamic culture.

The aim of the final product was to make it light, cheap and small. It's printed on a very thin recycled paper and is binded without spine. The format of the magazine is slightly tight, to remind as a storybook.
